Tropics

The Tropics are regions surrounding the Earth's equator, characterized by warm temperatures year-round and high levels of rainfall. These areas host lush rainforests, diverse wildlife, and vibrant ecosystems. Known for their breathtaking beaches and rich biodiversity, tropical regions are perfect for exploring natural wonders. However, they also face challenges like deforestation and climate change, which threaten their delicate balance.
